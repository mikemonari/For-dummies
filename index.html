HOW IT WORKS

How to sign-up: Its as easy as 1-2-3

01   Visit the Website

Open your web browser and type in the URL https://juvenotes.live. Press "Enter" to access the site. Once you are in the website, Press button STUDY NOW.This will take you to the portal to sign up.

02   Create an Account

Look for "Create an Account" button located at the bottom of the page, press the button and Fill in the required fields with accurate information and press button Create my new account

03   Log in

You can now log in to the website using your username and password to access your new account.

ABOUT US

"Born from the passion and drive of fellow medical students, our platform stands as a testament to the adage, 'By the students, for the students. ' We navigated the challenging corridors of medical school and recognized a significant void when it came to comprehensive, student-friendly resources. Rather than waiting for solutions, we took the stethoscope into our own hands, crafting a space tailored to the unique needs and demands of medical academia. Our mission? To bridge the gap, ensuring every budding doctor has access to the tools they need to thrive. Dive in, and let's flourish together in this noble journey of healing and learning!"
